In the Age of AI

Return to Human

The things that make you feel most alive are the things no machine will ever touch.

Enter

We lost ourselves a bit.

Anxiety is skyrocketing. Depression is everywhere. We are more connected than ever and more alone than ever. We outsourced our attention, our bodies, our decision making to algorithms designed to keep us hooked and hollow. AI is changing everything. But here is the truth: turbulent waters are an invitation to find our deepest roots. The fire we are walking through right now is calling us home to what makes us actually, fiercely, undeniably human. This is not a retreat from the modern world. This is an ascent toward your truest power.

What No Algorithm Can Replicate

The feeling of singing in the shower when nobody is listening.

The feeling of making love.

The feeling of conquering a challenge you thought would break you.

The feeling of your child falling asleep on your chest.

The quiet knowing that you are exactly where you are meant to be.

The raw, burning aliveness of coming home to yourself.

The Guide

Anthony Balduzzi

Anthony Balduzzi lost his father at nine. That loss set him on a path through competitive bodybuilding, naturopathic medicine, a near fatal skiing accident, seven surgeries, plant medicine in the Amazon, a week in total darkness, and the slow return to what was always there. He is not a guru. He is a man who walked the fire and came back to tell you: the way home is through, not around.
