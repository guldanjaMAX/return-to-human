Speak

Anthony Balduzzi speaks on the intersection of physical mastery, spiritual depth, and what it means to be fully alive in the age of AI.

Speaking Topics

The Through Line of Pain and Purpose

How every wound is preparation. Anthony's story from losing his father to finding God, and what it teaches about the path through suffering to power.

Physical Alignment as a Gateway to Spiritual Alignment

The body is not separate from the soul. How training the physical form opens doorways to connection, clarity, and communion with Creator.

Return to Human: Finding Ourselves in the Age of AI

The flagship talk. What happens when machines handle everything but the things that make us feel alive? A call to adventure, embodiment, and sacred reconnection.

Sacred Plant Medicine and Men's Transformation

The role of ceremony, nature, and sacred substances in breaking through the walls men build around their hearts. Not recreational. Sacred.

Facing Fear as the Gateway to Full Expression

How accomplished men lose themselves in success, and the radical path back to aliveness through confronting the things they have been avoiding.
